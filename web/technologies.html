Season /crop

Maize can be cultivated throughout the year in almost all the states of India. The purpose of cultivation may vary from the use as grain, fodder, green cobs, sweet corn, baby corn, popcorn. The highest maize production stated in India are given below.

Crops States
Wheat Uttar Pradesh, Punjab and Haryana
Gram Madhya Pradesh and Tamil Nadu
Barley Maharashtra, Uttar Pradesh and Rajasthan
Bajra Maharashtra, Gujarat and Rajasthan
Rice West Bengal, Andhra Pradesh, Chhattisgarh and Tamil Nadu

Maize is regarded as the third most important food crop in India. The first place is occupied by Rice and the second place by wheat respectively. The total production of maize is around 22.23 tonnes. It is contributing to 9% of national production. Maize can be considered as the basic raw material and ingredient for industrial products like protein, alcoholic beverages, food sweeteners, pharmaceutical, cosmetic, film, textile, gum, package, paper industries starch, oil etc.
