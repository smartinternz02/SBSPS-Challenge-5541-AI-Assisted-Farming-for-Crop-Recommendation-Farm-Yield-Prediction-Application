Crop Season

Temperature: Between 22-32°C with high humidity. Rainfall: Around 150-300 cm. Soil Type: Deep clayey and loamy soil. Top Rice Producing States: West Bengal > Punjab > Uttar Pradesh > Andhra Pradesh > Bihar. It is the staple food crop of majority of Indian people. India is the second largest producer of rice in the world after China.
